TULASI MULTI SPECALITY HOSPITAL

ICU critical care requires well trained nurses, respiratory therapists and physicians to attend to patients almost every minute of every hour of every day. Less well staffed hospitals, more prone to burn-out and absenteeism, will be less able to provide that high level of continuous care that for some patients needs to go on for weeks or even months. · One of the big learnings of treating very ill Covid-19 patients is that they usually benefit from being “proned” – or turned on their stomachs. You can imagine how dicey a procedure it is to turn an adult patient who is intubated with multiple intravenous lines and catheters. Less well staffed hospitals simply don’t have the trained person power to turn critically ill patients frequently and safely enough.
